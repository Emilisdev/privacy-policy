
Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

When using the app, we may collect the following information:
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Username (nickname) provided by you
- User ID generated by Supabase Authentication
- Basic device and usage data through Firebase Analytics

If you use the app as a guest, we do not collect personal identification data such as email or username. However, anonymous usage data may still be collected unless you decline analytics.

How We Use Information

We use collected data to:
- Create and manage user accounts
- Provide login and authentication functionality
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns through analytics

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services provided by Google Firebase, including:
- Supabase Authentication
- Firebase Analytics

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Analytics and Consent

Analytics data is only collected if you provide consent. You can choose not to allow analytics tracking when using the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time within the app.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.
